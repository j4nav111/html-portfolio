The Best Movies According to Janavi

My top 3 favourite movies/genres of all time!!

1. Scary Movie(All the sequels!)

These movies are paradoies of the original horror movies. They are extremely funny to watch and always know how to lighten up your mood after a bad day.

2. Any old bollywood movie(2000s-2015)

These movies are heart warming and always end with a deep life lesson or a valuable lesson like, "We only live once.." or "Family is important".
They are very emotional, a few examples are 'Kuch Kuch Hota Hai', 'YJHD', 'ZNMD', etc.

Horror Movies

I get extremely scared watching them but yet they are very fun to watch. Any horror movie is a good horror movie especially a one with sequels.
